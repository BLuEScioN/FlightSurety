Airline Actions

  1. Register up to 3 airlines (because once there are 4, mutli-party consensus kicks in and airlines have to vote to register new airlines)
  2. Fund airline 1. Fund from account 1 and try funding from another account
  3. Try to register a flight with this airline (flights are hard-coded and selected from on the FE)
  4. Try to register a flight with airline 2, which has not been funded. Should throw error.
  5. Register a 4th airline. Try to fund it. Try to register a flight. Vote the airline into registration.
